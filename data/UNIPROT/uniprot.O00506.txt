CC       to the Golgi apparatus where it appears to regulate protein
CC       transport events, cell adhesion, and polarity complexes important
CC       for cell migration.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: ATP + a protein = ADP + a phosphoprotein.

CC       of stabilization of dimer formation. The C-terminal non-catalytic
CC       region inhibits the kinase activity.
