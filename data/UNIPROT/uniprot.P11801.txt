CC   -!- FUNCTION: May be a SFC-associated serine kinase (splicing factor
CC       compartment-associated serine kinase) with a role in intranuclear
CC       SR protein (non-snRNP splicing factors containing a
CC       serine/arginine-rich domain) trafficking and pre-mRNA processing.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: ATP + a protein = ADP + a phosphoprotein.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Activity depends on Ca(2+) concentration.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Golgi apparatus. Cytoplasm, cytoskeleton,
CC       centrosome. Nucleus speckle. Endoplasmic reticulum membrane;
CC       Lipid-anchor. Cell membrane; Lipid-anchor. Cytoplasm.
CC       Note=Localized in the brefeldin A-sensitive Golgi compartment, at
CC       centrosomes, in the nucleus with a somewhat speckle-like presence,
CC       membrane-associated to the endoplasmic reticulum (ER) and the
CC       plasma membrane (PM), and more diffusely in the cytoplasm. Found
CC       to concentrate in splicing factor compartments (SFCs) within the
CC       nucleus of interphase cells. The acylation-negative form may be
CC       only cytoplasmic and nuclear. Acylation seems to allow the
CC       sequestering to the intracellular membranes. Myristoylation may
CC       mediate targeting to the intracellular non-Golgi membranes and
CC       palmitoylation may mediate the targeting to the Golgi membranes.
CC       Dual acylation is required to stabilize the interaction with Golgi
CC       membranes.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in all tissues and cell lines tested
CC       with the highest level of abundance in testis.
CC   -!- PTM: Autophosphorylated on serine residues.
CC   -!- PTM: Myristoylated. Required for membrane association.
CC       Prerequisite for palmitoylation to occur.
CC   -!- PTM: Palmitoylated.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the protein kinase superfamily. CAMK
CC       Ser/Thr protein kinase family.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Contains 1 protein kinase domain.
