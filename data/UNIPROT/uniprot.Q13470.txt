CC       tumor suppressor properties. Plays a negative regulatory role in
CC       the Ras-MAPK pathway. May function in signaling pathways utilized
CC       broadly during fetal development and more selectively in adult
CC       tissues and in cells of the lymphohematopoietic system. Could
CC       specifically be involved in phospholipid signal transduction.
